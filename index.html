#Our Story

2 Decades, One Heart: from Our Home to a Sanctuary

For over 20 years, our home has been more than just a house; it has been a hospital, a nursery, and a refuge.

We didn't start as an organization; we started as two people who couldn't look away from an animal in need. We have operated entirely from our own pockets—funding medical care, food, and shelter for hundreds of animals.

The Turning Point

To save more lives, we needed to build something bigger than ourselves. We decided it was time to transition from a private rescue into a public sanctuary.

#Who We Are

WHO ARE WE?

Founder: Fiorenza Krejza

An Italian/Ghanaian citizen residing in Ghana since 2003. For over 20 years, she has rescued hundreds of sick and injured cats and dogs, personally financing all medical and feeding costs. She currently hosts over 50 cats and 3 dogs in her home.

Co-founder: Magdalene Ayensu

A psychology graduate from Winneba University. She manages 54 cats and one dog. To protect them, she took a loan to build a temporary shelter where she also sleeps to prevent the animals from being stolen at night.

The Vision for the Future

We are building a permanent, sustainable facility designed to house:

  • 100+ cats in a safe environment.
  • 4 resident dogs as loyal guardians.

Moving from "saving them in our home" to "building them a home of their own."

VISION

To lead a movement of compassion across Ghana where every domestic animal has a place to call home.

MISSION

To provide a permanent sanctuary of safety, healing, and lifelong care for abandoned and vulnerable animals.

Financial Contribution

Direct donations go straight toward construction materials, veterinary bills, and daily nutrition.

  • One-Time Gift: every cedi/dollar/euro helps us to buy a brick or a bag of life-saving kibble.
  • Monthly Sponsorship: become a "sanctuary guardian" by providing consistent monthly support that ensures our residents never go hungry.
  • The "Brick by Brick" Fund: specifically for the construction of our new facility.
Supply Donation

Maintaining a sanctuary requires more than just funds—we are always in need of physical supplies.

  • Food: high quality dry and wet food for cats and dogs.
  • Medical Supplies: flea/tick treatment, dewormers, and first-aid kits.
  • Comfort: blankets, towels, cat trees, and sturdy toys for our four dogs.
  • Cleaning Supplies: safe, non-toxic disinfectants to keep our environment healthy.
Sponsor a Resident

Don't have room at home? You can "adopt" one of our residents virtually!

How it works: choose a specific cat or one of our 4 dogs. Your monthly sponsorship covers their specific medical and dietary needs, and we'll send you regular updates on your "furry godchild".

Spread the Word (the Power of Advocacy)

You don't need to spend a cent to make a difference.

  • Social Media: share our stories on Facebook and Instagram (Sanctuary / Aba's Rescue).
  • Community: tell your friends and family about the work we are doing in Ghana.
  • Connect Us: do you know a local business or vet clinic that might want to partner with us? Let us know!
