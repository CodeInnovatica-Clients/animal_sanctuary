Our Story

#Our Story

From Private Rescue to a Permanent Sanctuary

What started as a personal commitment in our own homes has grown into a community of over 100 rescued dogs and cats. While we provide them with love and safety, our current capacity is at its limit.

We are now transitioning to a Permanent Sanctuary. This facility will not only be a home for the homeless but a beacon of hope for animal welfare in Ghana.
